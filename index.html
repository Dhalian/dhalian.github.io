PS2 Guide

The complete guide to softmodding your PlayStation 2

There are a lot of methods to softmod your PS2, choose the right one according to your model and what accesories you have.

What is Homebrew ?

Homebrew applications are unlicensed software made for closed systems such as the PlayStation 2. These applications can range from utilities to custom homebrew games

What is FreeMCBoot ?

FreeMCBoot is an open source Homebrew loader for PS2, you launch it from a MagicGate Memory Card, it can be installed with other methods such as FreeDVDBoot, FreeHDBoot

What can i do by modding my system ?

Launch PS2 ISOs from a USB Drive using OPL(OpenPS2Loader).

Use emulators such as RetroArch.

Play region-locked games.
