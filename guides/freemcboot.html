PS2 Guide - FreeMCBoot Section

Section I - 1
Prerequisites

To install FreeMCBoot from your SD Card : you'll need :

  1. A PS2
  2. A USB Drive, formatted in FAT 32
    • To format your USB Drive, the recommended tool is GUIFormat
  3. A Memory Card (MagicGate) with FreeMCBoot preinstalled
Section I - 2
Preparing your files
  1. Download the AIO Pack
  2. Extract the AIO to the root of your USB Drive
  3. Eject the USB Drive from your device
  4. Plug the USB Device into the first USB Port of your console
  5. Launch your PS2
Section I - 3
Installing FreeMCBoot

By this step you are going to install FreeMCBoot onto your Memory Card, you will also format your Memory Card, don't forget to backup your saves data !

  1. Launch your PS2
  2. Launch the "uLaunchELF" sotfware
  3. In uLaunchELF, launch the "FreeMCBoot installer"
